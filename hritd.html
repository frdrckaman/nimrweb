Health Research, Information Technology and Documentation (HRITD) Department

Health Research, Information Technology and Documentation (RITD) department was established as one among the five departments of NIMR – Muhimbili Centre to facilitate the management of centres’ health research information generated, documentation and information technology affairs. Currently, the department has 4 staffs; Head of Department, 1 Statistician, 1 IT personnel and 1 medical research scientist.

Overall department responsibilities are;

  • 01. Documentation for the overall centre reports (Quarterly and Bi-annual reports) for submission to the NIMR headquarters and Ministry of health
  • 02. Organizing an archiving system including archiving centre documents including reports and publications made by staff members
  • 03. Management of a long-term information storage strategy
  • 04. Documentation of the lessons learned and success stories of the centre
  • 05. Retrieving documents upon request.
  • 06. With technology playing an increasingly important role in the development and success of modern businesses, it’s critical that IT teams should ensure that;
    • • All employees have the technology they need to get their job done, from a reliable working devices and VPN access, to up-close coordination with other departments like HR or finance for data protection and security
    • • Running regular checks on network and data security
    • • Identifying and acting on opportunities to improve and update software and systems
    • • Conducting regular system audits
    • • Running and sharing regular operation system reports with senior staff
    • • Overseeing and determining timeframes for IT projects including system updates, upgrades, migrations and outages
    • • Plan, organize, control and evaluate IT and electronic data operations.
  • 07. Mentor and supervise staff
